# 🌱 AgroShield AI  
### Smart Agriculture Risk Prediction & Farmer Alert System

🔗 **Live Project:**  
https://agroshieldai.netlify.app/

AgroShield AI is a smart agriculture platform that predicts **crop risks caused by rainfall, pests, and environmental conditions** using intelligent analytics.

The system helps farmers **identify potential threats early and take preventive actions**, reducing crop losses and improving farm productivity.

This project was developed for the **Tamil Nadu Hackathon for Women in Science & Engineering (TNWISE 2026)** under the **Smart Agriculture** theme.

---

# 🚜 Problem Statement

Farmers face significant crop damage due to:

- 🌧 Sudden heavy rainfall  
- 🐛 Rapid pest multiplication  
- 🦗 Unexpected locust swarms  
- 📉 Lack of integrated crop risk analysis systems  

Currently, there is **no simple, affordable, and integrated prediction platform** available for small-scale farmers.

AgroShield AI aims to solve this problem by providing **early crop risk prediction and smart alerts**.

---

# 🎯 Project Goals

- Predict **crop risks early** using weather and environmental data
- Provide **timely alerts and recommendations** to farmers
- Use **Artificial Intelligence for smart farming decisions**
- Reduce **crop losses caused by climate changes and pests**
- Improve **agricultural productivity with data-driven insights**

---

# ⚙️ How It Works

### 1️⃣ Data Collection
Collects important environmental data such as:

- Rainfall
- Temperature
- Humidity
- Soil conditions

### 2️⃣ Data Processing
The collected data is cleaned and analyzed to identify patterns related to crop risks.

### 3️⃣ Risk Prediction
The system analyzes the processed data to predict possible threats such as:

- Rain damage
- Pest attacks
- Locust outbreaks

### 4️⃣ Alert System
Farmers receive **early alerts and preventive recommendations** so they can take timely action.
